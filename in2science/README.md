# Mapping Gentrification

## 1. Get the Data

I’ve (partially) prepared some data for you so that you don’t have to track everything down yourselves. There are two files you need:

1. [London LSOA boundaries](https://github.com/jreades/talks/raw/gh-pages/in2science/data/LSOA_2011_London.zip)
2. [LSOA-level Census Data](https://github.com/jreades/talks/raw/gh-pages/in2science/data/LSOA_2011_London.zip) on:
    - Median House Prices in 2001 & 2011
    - Median Household Income in 2001 & 2011
    - Percentage with Level 4+ NVQ Qualifications in 2001 & 2011
    - Percentage of ‘Knowledge Workers’ in 2001 & 2011 (Professionals & Managers)

I would suggest moving these to your ‘Documents’ folder before double-clicking them to unzip them into files.
