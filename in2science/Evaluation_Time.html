Evaluation Time!

Part 1. Post-it Notes (~15 minutes)

Working as a group out post-it notes and organise them however you like to reflect on the following questions about the past week:

  1. What did you love?
  2. What did you learn?
  3. What would you change?
  4. What would you drop?

We want the change to learn from you in the same way that you (hopefully) learned from us!

Part 2. Marketing Meeting (~30 minutes)

Drawing on your answers to Part 1, explore the following: if you were in CASA and wanted to market our In2scienceUK placement opportunity to next year’s A-level students (either as part of In2science or separately), how would you do it? What would you highlight? How would you reach them? What would you say?

Part 3. The Future (~10 minutes)

Is there anything you’ve done this week that has changed your thinking about the future? Is there something you’d like to explore further as part of a hobby, or even a degree? Basically, is there a direction in which you’d like to take one or more new ideas?

Part 4. Write-Up! (~1 hour)

Using the other three parts, can you collectively write (and illustrate–photos or drawings or maps) something suitable for use on the CASA blog?

Part 5. Present! (~20 minutes)

Summarise all of the above in just 15 minutes! 😁
